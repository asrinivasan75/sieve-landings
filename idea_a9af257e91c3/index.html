The flow

Three steps, then you're eating

Step 1
Snap the meal
Open the app, point your camera at whatever you're about to eat — plated, in a container, on a restaurant table — and tap once.
Step 2
Review the log
The app returns identified food items, calories, protein, carbs, and fat within seconds. You can adjust any number before confirming.
Step 3
Plan adapts
Your weekly meal plan and 4-day workout split update based on how closely you're hitting your targets — so the plan stays realistic, not aspirational.

The difference we're betting on is recognition accuracy on unstructured meals — home-cooked food, restaurant plates without labels.
